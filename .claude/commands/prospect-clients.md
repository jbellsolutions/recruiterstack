Generate personalized client outreach sequences for business development.

1. Check config exists. If not, run /setup.
2. Ask for target: company name, industry, location, or broad criteria (e.g., "tech companies in Austin hiring engineers").
3. Research the target company using available data or ask the user for context.
4. Identify likely decision-makers: VP Talent, Director of Recruiting, HR Director, Hiring Manager.
5. Generate a 3-email sequence:
   - Email 1 (Day 0): Pattern interrupt — specific observation about their hiring + how you can help
   - Email 2 (Day 3): Proof — relevant case study or result from a similar company
   - Email 3 (Day 8): Soft close — door's open, no pressure
6. All emails: under 80 words, professional but warm, signed by the agency owner's name from config.
7. Display the full sequence with subject lines.
8. Ask: "Want to adjust the tone? Generate for more companies? Or copy these to your outreach tool?"

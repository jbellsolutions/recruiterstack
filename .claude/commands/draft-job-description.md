Generate a professional job description from intake call notes or role description.

1. Ask the user to paste their intake call notes or describe the role in their own words.
2. Extract key details: title, department, location, salary/rate range, must-have skills, nice-to-haves, responsibilities, team size, reporting structure, company culture notes.
3. Generate a polished JD with:
   - Compelling opening paragraph that sells the opportunity
   - Key Responsibilities (5-7 bullets)
   - Requirements: Must-Haves vs Nice-to-Haves (clearly separated)
   - Compensation and Benefits (if provided)
   - Company Overview (if available)
4. Display the JD and ask for revisions.
5. Offer: "Want to post this to your ATS?" or "Save as a file?"

Tone: professional, inclusive language, avoids jargon. Focus on what the candidate GETS (growth, impact, team) not just what's required.
